Chicken Cacciatore

Description

A dish that translates to Hunter's Chicken, this is an easy and delicious recipe that incorporates chicken, tomato and bell peppers. Optionally you can includes ingredients such as capers, olives, onion and other vegetables that you have on hand.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. skin-on chicken thighs
  • 3-4 ripe tomatoes, cored and halved
  • 1 onion, peeled and halved
  • 1-2 bell peppers, de-seeded and halved
  • 3-4 cloves garlic
  • olive oil
  • black olives (optional)
  • capers (optional)

Steps

  1. Turn on broiler.
  2. In a large baking pan, add chicken thighs, tomatoes, onions and peppers. Coat with oil and season with salt and pepper. Arrange vegetables with skin side up.
  3. Add garlic to the baking, but add the cloves under the chicken. Place the pan under the broiler.
  4. Allow the vegetables to char under the broiler (approx. 20-30 minutes). Flip the chicken halfway through.
  5. When the outer surface of the chicken appears cooked and juices from the tomatoes have released, remove the baking pan from the oven. Switch the oven to bake at about 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  6. Removed charred peel from the tomatoes and bell peppers. Separate the vegetables from the chicken, and mash the tomatoes, onions and peppers into a sauce. Add olives and capers if desired.
  7. Pile sauce on top of chicken thighs and return the pan to the oven. Allow the sauce to thicken by boiling excess water (approx. 20 minutes).
  8. Add balsamic vinegar and/or herbs to taste. Serve over polenta, rice or potatoes.
